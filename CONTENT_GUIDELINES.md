## üéØ Mission Statement

Glad Tidings is a medical missionary health and wellness platform dedicated to providing natural health remedies, spiritual wellness insights, and holistic healing guidance to our community.

## üìù Content Standards

### **Tone & Voice**
- **Professional yet approachable** - Medical expertise with compassionate care
- **Spiritually grounded** - Faith-based perspective on health and wellness
- **Educational** - Informative content that empowers users
- **Encouraging** - Supportive and uplifting messaging
- **Respectful** - Honoring diverse beliefs and health journeys

### **Language Guidelines**

#### ‚úÖ **Preferred Terms**
- "Natural health remedies"
- "Holistic wellness"
- "Spiritual health"
- "Medical missionary services"
- "Herbal remedies"
- "Wellness practices"
- "Health consultations"
- "Natural healing"
- "Spiritual wellness"
- "Community health"

#### ‚ùå **Avoid These Terms**
- "Hello/Hey/Hi" (use "Greetings" or "Welcome")
- "Awesome/Cool/Dude" (use "Excellent/Wonderful/Friend")
- "Gonna/Wanna/Kinda" (use "Going to/Want to/Kind of")
- Casual slang or overly informal language
- Medical claims without proper disclaimers
- Promises of cures or guaranteed results

### **Content Categories**

#### **1. Health & Wellness Articles**
- Focus on natural remedies and preventive care
- Include scientific backing where appropriate
- Provide practical, actionable advice
- Always include disclaimer: "Consult with healthcare professionals"

#### **2. Spiritual Health Content**
- Faith-based perspectives on wellness
- Prayer and meditation guidance
- Spiritual practices for healing
- Biblical health principles

#### **3. Product Descriptions**
- Accurate, honest descriptions
- Focus on benefits and uses
- Include ingredients and sourcing information
- Transparent about limitations and contraindications

#### **4. Community Stories**
- Testimonials with proper permissions
- Success stories and journeys
- Volunteer experiences
- Community impact reports

## üìã Content Review Checklist

### **Before Publishing:**
- [ ] Tone is professional and compassionate
- [ ] No casual language or slang
- [ ] Medical claims are properly disclaimed
- [ ] Content aligns with medical missionary values
- [ ] Grammar and spelling are correct
- [ ] Formatting is consistent and readable
- [ ] Links are working and relevant
- [ ] Images are appropriate and high-quality

### **Quality Standards:**
- **Accuracy:** All health information is medically sound
- **Clarity:** Easy to understand for general audience
- **Compassion:** Empathetic and supportive tone
- **Integrity:** Honest and transparent communication
- **Relevance:** Valuable to target audience

## üé® Visual Content Guidelines

### **Images:**
- Professional, high-quality photos
- Natural lighting and authentic settings
- Diverse representation of people
- Medical and wellness themes
- Spiritual imagery where appropriate

### **Colors:**
- Primary: Emerald green (#10b981)
- Secondary: Soft blues and earth tones
- Accent: Warm golds and soft whites
- Avoid: Harsh, neon, or overly bright colors
